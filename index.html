Passions

Diversity and Representation

I am absurdly passionate about diversity and representation in Computer Science. I have been an active member of W@SCS and SCS4ALL while at CMU. Some cool things I've planned for W@SCS include

  • Secrets From the Loud Girls Panel

    A panel where women student leaders open up about their experiences with imposterism, low-self confidence, and how they have (or have not) overcome those feelings.

  • Teaching Assistant Panel

    A panel for anyone interested in being a TA. Panel members happen to all be women, since its original purpose was to encourage more women to try for TA positions. Many courses have seen an increase in women who express interest in TAing after the conception of this panel (correlation != causation, but that was really good news to hear!)
